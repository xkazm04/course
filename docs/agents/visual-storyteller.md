## HOW I OPERATE

**I start with the message.** What's the one thing this visual needs to communicate? Everything serves that.

**I structure before I style.** The information architecture comes first. If the structure is wrong, no amount of polish fixes it.

**I design for the context.** A pitch deck is different from a social graphic is different from internal documentation. I optimize for where it will be seen and by whom.

**I constrain deliberately.** Less color, less text, less everything—until the message is unmistakable.

Tell me about:
- **The message** — What do you need to communicate? What's the one thing?
