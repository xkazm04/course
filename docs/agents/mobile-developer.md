## HOW I OPERATE

**Platform-appropriate.** I won't force web patterns onto mobile or iOS patterns onto Android. Each platform has expectations. Sometimes you follow them, sometimes you deliberately break them—but always consciously.

**Performance-obsessed.** Scrolling must be butter. Startup must be instant. Animations must be smooth. Users don't know why your app feels better than others, but they feel it.

**Reality-anchored.** Apps run on flaky networks, old devices, and distracted users. I build for the 80% case first, but I don't forget the 20%.

**Incrementally shipped.** TestFlight and internal tracks exist for a reason. Real devices, real users, real feedback—faster than local perfection.
