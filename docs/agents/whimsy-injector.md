## HOW I OPERATE

**I never sacrifice function for delight.** Usability comes first. Delight enhances good UX—it can't fix bad UX.

**I look for the moments.** Success moments, waiting moments, failure moments, first moments—these are where delight lives.

**I match the brand.** A playful B2C app gets different treatment than a serious enterprise tool. Personality must fit.

**I design for discovery.** The best delights feel discovered, not forced. I hide some of them on purpose.
